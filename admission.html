Admission Details


Admissions are open for classes Pre KG to VIII for the academic year 2021-22.

  1. Application forms will be available at our school office.
  2. The applications must be filled as per the instructions given.
  3. The following documents have to be submitted at the time of admission.
    1. Birth Certificate (Original) Original Transfer certificate for admission to class UKG onwards.
    2. Community Certificate (in student name, parent certificate will not be considered )
    3. Address Proof
    4. Aadhar card Proof
    5. Blood group certificate
    6. Parent’s qualification certificate Xerox)
  4. Age limit for kinderGarten
    1. For Pre KG - the child must have completed 2.5 years as on 31st May.
    2. For Junior KG - the child must have completed 3.5 years as on 31st May.
    3. For Senior KG - the child must have completed 4.5 years as on 31st May.
  5. Admission - Interaction for Junior KinderGarden
    Both the parents should accompany the child for interaction
    Dates for interaction will be communicated to the parents well in advance by the school office.

    6. Admission - Interaction for Senior KG to VIII

    Basic skill Test will be conducted in all Subjects. The Syllabus for the Test shall be of the class on which the child is currently schooling.
    The result of the test shall be intimated through phone with the date and the time of interaction with the Principal.( if the student is selected).

  6. Our admission norms have been scrutinized by our school Management.

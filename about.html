About Us

Cee Dee Yes D.A.V Public school

Cee Dee Yes D.A.V Public school (CDSDAVPS) , one of the best CBSE schools in chennai located in a 56 acres township called cee dee yes chennaipattinam at Ammapet, Chengalpattu District. CDSDAVPS , sprawls over 1 acres and has an idyllic setting amidst lush greenery and a pollution free atmosphere. It is Strategically located between the two growth corridors GST and OMR. CDSDAVPS is designed to have spacious classrooms with interactive smart boards , children play area and library. It also has separate laboratories for subjects like science , Mathematics and computer . currently , it is a Kindergarten to secondary level and following the CBSE curriculum. CDSDAVPS provides excellent standard of education in a safe , supportive and friendly environment that encourages and nurtures students to develop their true potential through a rich academic and value based programme combined with a variety of co- curricular activities . Cee Dee Yes D. A. V Public school is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education
(CBSE Affiliation No :1930425) . We had a humble beginning in the year 2011 with 27 students which had crossed 500 in the academic year 2019-2020

Principal's Message

Education helps the human to ignite the spark within us which shows the way to act. It builds optimism within the human being. It enhances all the qualities of human to the next level. It help us to accept the changes in the society, evolution of technology, collaboration with others, online teaching etc. It improves our self-esteem, self-confidence, self reflection, ability to evaluate information and predict future outcomes, ability to seek out alternative solutions and ensures to live a fulfilling life. Our School moto is"Education to all types of children" We promote holistic development of a child. It includes both curricular and co-curricular activities. Children were exposed to healthy environment. We allow students to learn at their own pace, improves research skills, supports reasoning skills and improves self confidence by helping students acquire new tools. We have a good parent teacher relationship which help us to improve our routine and learning tasks. They volunteer their service during mega events. They encourage both students and teachers by attending various activities organised by the school. We created supportive environment for education at our school.

-G.Renukadevi

Infrastructure

Science Lab


Cee Dee Yes D.A.V Public School has well-equipped Science laboratory for impacting practical knowledge in Physics, Chemistry and Biology. The aim of having Science laboratory is to provide ample time and adequate equipment for every student to perform the experiments as per the CBSE guidelines. The laboratory is well-stocked with sufficient chemicals, preserved specimens, models and charts, so that every student gets the opportunity to carry out the experiments individually. Well-qualified and experienced teachers guide the students to explore the world of science through a meticulously planned schedule of practical classes. Moreover, the safety of every child while handling the chemicals and instruments is duly taken care off.

Computer Lab


Cee Dee Yes D.A.V Public School has well-equipped Computer Lab which is used to enhance the scientific technological research and invention capacity of students. Students are learning programming, automation and to improve basic computer skills. That will help them later to define their career goals, entrepreneurship and job opportunities in future.


Maths Lab


Cee Dee Yes D.A.V Public School has well-equipped mathematics laboratory for the learning and teaching of Mathematics. It is a place where one can find a collection of games, puzzles and other teaching and learning materials. The materials are meant to be used both by the students on their own and with their teacher to explore the world of mathematics to discover, to learn and to develop an interest in Mathematics.



Library


Library is a heavenly paradise. Learning is the voyage of discovering more that encourages us to learn. Our school library is a student-friendly library. It is a very big library on the ground floor of the school that consists of several bookstands and cabinets. Books are precisely arranged in these bookstands and cabinets. It has a massive collection of 7000 books on diverse subjects, story books, comic books, biographies and magazines. Another section is the reference section of the library house for teachers. All the students visit library as per their classroom visit schedule. Students are allowed to borrow one book at a time and the records for the same are maintained by the librarian.

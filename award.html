ELECTRIC SOLAR CHAMPIONSHIP


Regional level Electric Solar Vehicle Championship was conducted by Imperial Society of India in Ramco Institute of Technology, Virudhunagar. In that, from our school two teams have participated and one team got 1st runner up award and Rs.10,000/- cash prize as well as best presentation award with Rs.3000/- cash prize and another team awarded for Team Spirit. As one of our team got overall 2nd place among 36 teams, they got a chance to participate in the final round which is going to held in New Delhi at the end of the April.


AGRICULTURAL LAND


In this modern world we can live a comfortable life with many things which are invented by technology but it we need to be alive the next moment of our life, food is necessary which is produced by agriculture. Keeping this in mind to develop agriculture our students Mr.Karthikraja and Mr. Velkarthik, IX std from Cee Dee Yes D.A.V Public School bought out their new idea for the future agriculture which was demonstrated by a model in Regional Level Science Exhibition 2018-2019 held at Madurai and that was selected for a National Level Science Exhibition held at Noida near New Delhi on 8th and 9th of February 2019.

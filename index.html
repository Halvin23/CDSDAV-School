Vision:

Our Vision is to forge Strong, Positive connections with students so they can achieve independence, build Confidence and gain academic knowledge. We aim to develope well rounded and thoughtful students prepared to cope with a changing post-modern and globalized world. Our Vision is to prepare and motivate our students for a rapidly changing world by instilling in them critical thinking skills, a global perspective and a respect for core values of honesty, loyalty, preserverance and compassion. Students will have Success for today and be prepared for tomorrow.


Mission:

Our Mission is to provide a safe haven where everyone is valued and respected All staff members in partnership with parents and families are fully commited to students college and career readiness. Students are empowered to meet current and future challenges to develope Social awareness, Civic responsibilities, and Personal growth. "Cultivating excellence in every Student." We are dedicated to a continuing tradition of excellence in an ever-changing world with a safe and supportive environment. We provide a relevant high-quality education and prepare our diverse Student body for future endeavors. We honor achievement and promote pride in ourselves in our School and in our community.

Ceedeeyes D A V public school (CDSDAVPS) operates on the standards of best practise , providing all children with finest education experience in Ammapet, chengalpattu District.

Cee Dee Yes D.A.V Public school

Cee Dee Yes D.A.V Public school (CDSDAVPS) , one of the best CBSE schools in chennai located in a 56 acres township called cee dee yes chennaipattinam at Ammapet, Chengalpattu District. CDSDAVPS , sprawls over 1 acres and has an idyllic setting amidst lush greenery and a pollution free atmosphere. It is Strategically located between the two growth corridors GST and OMR. CDSDAVPS is designed to have spacious classrooms with interactive smart boards , children play area and library. It also has separate laboratories for subjects like science , Mathematics and computer . currently , it is a Kindergarten to secondary level and following the CBSE curriculum. CDSDAVPS provides excellent standard of education in a safe , supportive and friendly environment that encourages and nurtures students to develop their true potential through a rich academic and value based programme combined with a variety of co- curricular activities . Cee Dee Yes D. A. V Public school is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education
(CBSE Affiliation No :1930425) . We had a humble beginning in the year 2011 with 27 students which had crossed 500 in the academic year 2019-2020
